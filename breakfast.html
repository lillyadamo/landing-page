Breakfast

The meal eaten in the morning. The English term refers to breaking the overnight fasting period. Traditions of eating morning meals have existed since ancient times. Dinner was actually the word used for breakfast, as it also refers to "Breaking a fast", but it's meaning shifted in the mid 13th century.
