Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A gourmet take on a great lunch classic!

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 inch slices of fresh sourdough bread
  • Approx. 6 thin slices camembert cheese (enough to cover two slices of bread)
  • Approx. 6 thin slices sharp, aged cheddar (enough to cover 2 slices of bread)
  • 4 tsp fresh thyme
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • fresh ground pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the sourdough bread into four 1/2 slices
  2. Top two slices of sourdough with slicesd camembert cheese. Next, sprinkle the thyme, lemon zest, and ground pepper over the camembert slices.
  3. On the other two slices of sourdough, add the slices of cheddar cheese. Carefully close the sandwichand press together with your hands.
  4. Heat skillet over medium heat and melt some butter in the pan
  5. Place the sandwich in the skillet. Cook for several minutes, pressing down with the spatula occasionally, until the cheese begins to melt and the underside of the bread is golden brown.
  6. Spread a bit more butter on the top of the sandwitch and carefully flip with a spatula, cook on this side for another few minutes until the underside is golden brown.
  7. Feel free to flip a few more times to ensure even cooking. Once the cheese has melted, remove from the pan and place onto a cutting board.
  8. Carefully cut the sandwiches in half and serve immediately!
