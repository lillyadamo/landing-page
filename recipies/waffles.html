Waffles

Who doesn't love waffles? They're easy to cook and the best part is that can be dressed up with anything you'd like!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3-1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 large eggs, separated, room temperature
  • 1-1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder. In another bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Add the milk, butter, and vanilla. Mix together well. Stir in the dry ingredients until just combined. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then fold gently into the batter.
  2. Bake in a preheated waffle maker according to the manufacturer's directions until golden brown
  3. Top with anything you'd like! Strawberries and whipping cream, chocolate drizzle, maple syrup, caramelized bananas, and blueberry sauce are just a few ideas.
